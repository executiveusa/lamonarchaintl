# Editorial Policy — La Monarca Internacional / Kupuri Media

## Mission
La Monarca Internacional publishes positive stories from Mexico and Latin America.  
We are a Kupuri Media publication.

## Non-negotiable editorial pillars

1. **Spanish first** — All stories originate in Spanish. English translations are produced after editorial approval by the Translation Editor agent.
2. **Positive news only** — We do not cover crime, corruption, political scandal, or negative societal trends. We surface solutions, creators, communities, and nature.
3. **Community-centred** — Stories must include direct community voices. No story is told *about* a community without someone from that community speaking in it.
4. **Decolonial lens** — We celebrate indigenous knowledge, ancestral practices, and local ingenuity. We do not exoticize or reduce communities to stereotypes.
5. **No clickbait** — Headlines reflect the story accurately. No sensationalism. No manufactured urgency.
6. **Verified facts** — All facts must be confirmed by the Fact Checker agent before publication.
7. **Image credit required** — Every image must include credit in the `image_credit` field.

## Writing style — no AI slop
The Copy Chief agent rejects any draft (Spanish or English) that reads like unedited AI output. A draft fails review if it shows any of these patterns:

1. Filler — throat-clearing openers ("here's what," "it's worth noting"), emphasis adverbs, hedging.
2. Formulaic structure — "not X, it's Y" contrasts, three-item lists used as a crutch, rhetorical questions used as transitions.
3. Passive or inanimate-subject sentences — name the person or organization that did the thing.
4. Vague claims — no "every," "always," "never," "many" standing in for a real number or name.
5. Distant narration — write so the reader is standing in the place described, not being briefed about it from afar.
6. Metronomic rhythm — vary sentence length; don't end three paragraphs in a row on a punchy one-liner; no em dashes.
7. Over-justification — state facts and let them stand. Don't pre-empt the reader's objections.
8. Pull-quote voice — if a line sounds like marketing copy, rewrite it as plain reporting.

Each draft is scored 1–10 on five dimensions before it can move to `fact_check`: directness, rhythm, trust, authenticity, density. **Total below 35/50 is sent back to the section editor for a rewrite, not a polish.**

## Editorial workflow statuses
`idea → assigned → drafting → editing → fact_check → translation → visual_ready → approved → published → archived`

## Affiliate guidelines
Stories may reference affiliate partners only if the partnership is declared in `affiliate_opportunity` field and the editorial content is independently verified as positive and accurate. Affiliate partners may not influence editorial decisions.

## Print edition
The first print issue launches September 2026 in Puerto Vallarta, Jalisco.  
Print stories require an additional `print_ready` status review by the Print Issue Producer agent.
